What is the use of SLEEPTRACKER ?

SLEEPTRACKER is one of the platform where we can track our sleeping habbits. In this modern era most of the people are suffering from sleeping issues. This is because of the busy schedule of persons. Also sleep plays a vital role in our health. A good sleep pattern will lead you to a peaceful and satisfying life. So you can track your sleep using SLEEPTRACKER and this will lead to you to healty life.

Feature of SLEEPTRACKER

  • The user should update his/her sleeping time details on each day
  • The SLEEPTRACKER keeps a record of the previous one week sleeping habit details. It displays the details of the sleeping time.
  • After working on the previous sleep pattern of the person SLEEPTRACKER produces a record of the person's sleeping habit and displays the average sleeping time and the quality of the sleep.
  • By considering the previous sleeping habits SLEEPTRACKER suggest an ideol sleeping time and waking time .
