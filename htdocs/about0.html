SLEEPTRACKER

Why SLEEPTRACKER ?

SLEEPTRACKER is a dynamic web-app that tracks your sleeping habits. In this technology-driven era, most of you are suffering from sleeping irregularities. Spending most of your day-time working on the screen, your mind really needs regular sleep to stay calm and healthy. But owing to your busy schedules, we know that you are not able to manage it all. Here, we come to your rescue. You can track your sleep-hours using SLEEPTRACKER

Features of SLEEPTRACKER

  • You can add your daily wake-up and sleep timings on our portal.
  • SLEEPTRACKER keeps a record of the previous one week sleeping-schedule details. It displays the details of the sleeping time.
  • After working on your previous sleep patterns,SLEEPTRACKER displays the average sleeping time and the quality of the sleep in a pictorial form.
  • Considering the previous sleeping habits,SLEEPTRACKER suggest an ideal sleeping time and waking-up time .
